Request to Delete Your Findlik Account and Personal Data

Welcome to the official data deletion support page for the Findlik App, developed by Waseem Ahmad.

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data from our systems, we offer a manual request process to ensure your information is securely removed.

📩 How to Request Deletion

Please send an email to: findflikdevelopers@gmail.com

Include the following details:

  • Your registered email or phone number
  • Subject: Account Deletion Request
  • Confirmation that you want your data deleted

🧹 What Will Be Deleted?

  • Your profile (name, email, phone)
  • Lost/found item entries
  • App preferences and metadata

🕒 Retention and Timeline

We typically delete data within 5 business days. No personal data is retained unless legally required.

🔒 Data Security

All data is handled securely and encrypted in transit. No data is shared with third parties.

🤝 Thank You

If you have questions, contact us at: findflikdevelopers@gmail.com
